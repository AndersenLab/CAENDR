Standard Tracks

The Genes and Transcripts tracks are displayed by default.

Genes

Shows the most updated gene models for the selected species.

Transcripts

Shows transcripts of genes.

Transposons

The transposon track shows calls for C. elegans only. Calls are from Laricchia et al. 2017 and list transposon type and isotype reference strains.

Divergent Regions

Hyper-divergent regions are shown for C. elegans only. Calls are from Lee et al. 2020. Please read this paper for a full description.

Two divergent tracks are available:

  • Divergent Regions Summary - Divergent region intervals across ANY of the wild strains are shown.
  • Divergent Regions Strain - Divergent region intervals for individual strains.
